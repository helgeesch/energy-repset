    Example 4: Comparing Representation Models¶

    Runs a single search to find the best 3-month selection, then applies three different representation models to the same winning selection: Uniform, KMedoids cluster-size, and Blended (soft assignment). Illustrates the R (representation) pillar of the framework.

    Script: examples/ex4_representation_models.py

    Pillar Component
    F FeaturePipeline (Stats + PCA)
    O WassersteinFidelity + CorrelationFidelity
    S ExhaustiveCombiGen(k=3)
    R UniformRepresentationModel + KMedoidsClustersizeRepresentation + BlendedRepresentationModel
    A ObjectiveDrivenCombinatorialSearchAlgorithm with WeightedSumPolicy
